About me

My name is Julian Isaza and I am a self taught 3D artist located in Bonn, Germany. I work with the 3D software Blender since 2018. Since then I have been able to learn Blender thanks to YouTube videos and a lot of try and error. My goal is to work in the filmindustry as a 3D modelling, texturing or lighting artist. Thanks to the Glasfachschule Rheinbach and friends of mine, I was able to work on several projekts as a leading artist. I really hope you like my work.
